## Appendix B: Clinical Research Summary

### Tinnitus
- **Study:** Attias et al. (1990) — "Efficacy of Self-Hypnosis for Tinnitus"
- **Result:** 73% reported reduced symptoms
- **Sample:** 36 males, ages 28-58
- **Duration:** 2 months

### Bruxism/Teeth Grinding
- **Connection:** Stress and anxiety reduction via hypnosis
- **Mechanism:** Jaw muscle relaxation, habit reprogramming
- **Evidence:** Multiple clinical studies on stress-related bruxism

### Hot Flashes/Menopause
- **Study:** Baylor University (2018)
- **Result:** Hypnosis comparable to HRT for hot flash reduction
- **Mechanism:** Thermoregulatory control via hypnotic suggestion

### Fibromyalgia
- **Result:** 57% pain reduction in clinical trials
- **Mechanism:** Pain perception modification, relaxation response

### Weight Loss
- **Study:** HYPNODIET randomized controlled trial (2022)
- **Result:** Significant improvement in eating behaviors
- **Mechanism:** Reduced food impulsivity, improved self-control
