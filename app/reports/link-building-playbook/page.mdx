# Link Building Playbook — AppStarFamily Portfolio

**Compiled by Scriptor · Research Date: March 2026**

---

## Executive Summary

Four apps. Four different niches. One unified link acquisition strategy.

The highest ROI moves aren't outreach campaigns or paid placements — they're the free, permanent submissions that most indie developers skip entirely. AlternativeTo alone offers six backlinks from a DA 71 domain for Lyrical, each one taking two minutes to claim. Government health sites (CDC, Smokefree.gov, Helsenorge.no) represent the longest-term authority play for PouchOut but carry the biggest SEO payoff in a YMYL niche where trust signals are everything.

Start with the five universal platforms — all four apps, one afternoon, done. Then work the niche directories by app. The outreach campaigns come last, once the foundational links are in place.

*DA scores throughout this document are indicative estimates. Verify with Ahrefs or Moz before prioritising outreach.*

## PouchOut

PouchOut operates in a YMYL (Your Money Your Life) niche. Google applies heightened scrutiny to health-adjacent content, which means authority links from government and medical sources carry disproportionate weight. The Scandinavian angle is an underutilised edge — 1177.se and Helsenorge.no have high DA, low competition for app listings, and a directly relevant audience.

![PouchOut Link Building Target Matrix](/reports/infographics/link-building-pouchout-targets.svg)

*PouchOut — Priority target sites by authority and acquisition difficulty*

### Health Directories

The most accessible entry points. Start here.

- **[MyFitnessPal](https://www.myfitnesspal.com/)** — Health app ecosystem; submit via their partner/integration pages
- **[Quit Now!](https://quitnow.app/)** — Cessation app community; cross-promotion opportunity
- **[Healthgrades](https://www.healthgrades.com/)** — Health resource listings; cessation tools section
- **[WebMD](https://www.webmd.com/)** — Health app reviews; reach out to editorial team for inclusion in cessation roundups

### Scandinavian Resources

Two of the highest-DA, lowest-competition opportunities in this playbook.

- **[1177.se](https://www.1177.se/)** — Swedish national health portal (DA 78). The snus/nicotine pouch category is uniquely relevant. Contact via their *Om 1177* editorial team. Target the "Sluta snusa / Sluta med nikotin" resource pages.
- **[Helsenorge.no](https://www.helsenorge.no/)** — Norwegian public health portal (DA 74). Smokeless tobacco cessation is a documented public health priority in Norway. Contact via [helsenorge.no/kontakt](https://www.helsenorge.no/kontakt/) with a short pitch positioning PouchOut as a cessation support tool.
- **[FHI.no](https://www.fhi.no/)** — Norwegian Institute of Public Health. Research-focused; harder to get but high-trust if successful.

### Government & Authority

These are month-two plays. The links are worth pursuing but require a proper editorial pitch, not a directory submission.

- **[CDC.gov](https://www.cdc.gov/)** — Target the tobacco/cessation resource pages. Pitch via the Office on Smoking and Health contact form. Lead with the app's clinical-style tracking methodology.
- **[Smokefree.gov](https://smokefree.gov/)** — Directly in-niche. Managed by the National Cancer Institute. App submissions accepted for their tools section — see the [SmokefreeTXT page](https://smokefree.gov/tools-tips/text-programs/smokefree-txt) for submission process context.
- **[BeTobaccoFree.HHS.gov](https://betobaccofree.hhs.gov/)** — HHS cessation resources hub. Contact via the site's feedback form.

### "Best Of" Lists

Target journalists and bloggers who write cessation/quitting roundups. Search Google for:

- `"best apps to quit nicotine pouches"` — claim any gap in existing lists
- `"best apps to quit dipping"` — adjacent niche, relevant audience
- `"quit zyn app"` — find articles without an app recommendation and pitch inclusion
- `site:reddit.com "quit zyn" "app"` — find Reddit threads to participate authentically

### Competitor Citation Targets

Sites currently linking to competing cessation apps that should also link to PouchOut:

- Any site linking to **Smoke Free** or **QuitNow** is a candidate for a "you should also mention PouchOut" pitch
- Check competitor backlinks via Ahrefs/Moz and identify resource pages, roundups, and editorial mentions

### Guest Posts

Target publications covering health, productivity, and quitting habits:

- **[Healthline.com](https://www.healthline.com/)** (DA 93) — Pitch to their contributor team. Angle: "What actually works when quitting nicotine pouches — a data-driven breakdown"
- **[Well+Good](https://www.wellandgood.com/)** — Wellness editorial; lower barrier than Healthline
- **[Psychology Today](https://www.psychologytoday.com/)** — Habit and addiction angle; contributor network is open

### Reddit Communities

Authentic participation, not spam. These communities are high-traffic and frequently referenced by Google:

- **[r/QuittingZyn](https://www.reddit.com/r/QuittingZyn/)** — Most relevant. Contribute genuinely, then mention PouchOut when directly helpful.
- **[r/StopSmoking](https://www.reddit.com/r/stopsmoking/)** — Broader cessation; nicotine pouch quitters present
- **[r/NicotinePouches](https://www.reddit.com/r/NicotinePouches/)** — Harm reduction angle; users often asking about quitting

## Hypnobirthing+

The hypnobirthing niche sits at an intersection of professional credibility and community trust. Links from doula and midwife organisations carry real E-E-A-T weight — a certified birth professional recommending an app is exactly the signal Google values here. Prioritise professional org listings before media outreach.

![Hypnobirthing+ Link Building Target Matrix](/reports/infographics/link-building-hypnobirthing-targets.svg)

*Hypnobirthing+ — Priority target sites by authority and acquisition difficulty*

### Pregnancy Directories

- **[BabyCenter](https://www.babycenter.com/)** (DA 89) — Target their app roundups and community boards. The editorial team produces "best pregnancy apps" guides regularly; pitch inclusion directly.
- **[The Bump](https://www.thebump.com/)** (DA 84) — App roundup content is a core format. Reach out to their editorial team with a short pitch + App Store screenshots.
- **[What to Expect](https://www.whattoexpect.com/)** (DA 89) — The most-recognised pregnancy brand globally. App section is maintained editorially.
- **[Motherly](https://www.mother.ly/)** (DA 68) — Modern parenting media with high engagement. More accessible than BabyCenter for pitches.

### Doula & Midwife Networks

Low competition, high trust. These are the Do Now targets.

- **[DONA International](https://www.dona.org/)** — World's largest doula certifying organisation. Submit via their [resources for doulas](https://www.dona.org/resources/) section. A listing here is a credibility signal that no amount of content marketing can replicate.
- **[CAPPA](https://cappa.net/)** — Childbirth and Postpartum Professional Association. Resource listings open to apps that support birth professionals.
- **[Midwifery Today](https://www.midwiferytoday.com/)** — Publication for midwives; accepts app and resource submissions for their practitioners' toolbox section.
- **[International Confederation of Midwives](https://www.internationalmidwives.org/)** — Global; harder to get but high authority.

### "Best Of" Lists

Search for and target:

- `"best hypnobirthing apps"` — get into every existing list, then create resources for lists that don't yet exist
- `"hypnobirthing app review"` — identify bloggers who haven't reviewed Hypnobirthing+ yet and pitch a review copy
- `site:pinterest.com "hypnobirthing"` — Pinterest boards link out to resources; a well-placed pin drives referral traffic

### Guest Posts

- **[Evidence Based Birth](https://evidencebasedbirth.com/)** (DA 61) — Research-focused birth education site with a passionate practitioner audience. Pitch a guest post: "The evidence for relaxation techniques during labour — and how digital tools are changing practice."
- **[Lamaze International Blog](https://www.lamazeinternational.org/)** (DA 56) — Childbirth education audience. Contributor pitches accepted.
- **[Healthline Parenthood](https://www.healthline.com/parenthood)** (DA 93) — The Parenthood vertical specifically covers pregnancy apps. Hard to place but worth pursuing.

## Lyrical

AlternativeTo is the single highest ROI link play in this entire playbook for Lyrical. Six competitors. Six pages. Six backlinks from DA 71. Each submission takes two minutes. Do this today.

![AlternativeTo — Highest ROI Link Play for Lyrical](/reports/infographics/link-building-lyrical-targets.svg)

*Lyrical — AlternativeTo competitor citation strategy*

### AlternativeTo Strategy

This is critical and must happen in Week 1.

Visit each competitor's AlternativeTo page and click "Add as alternative." Submit Lyrical with a one-line description for each. The links are permanent and cumulative.

- **[Musixmatch on AlternativeTo](https://alternativeto.net/software/musixmatch/)** — Add Lyrical as alternative
- **[Genius on AlternativeTo](https://alternativeto.net/software/genius/)** — Add Lyrical as alternative
- **[Shazam on AlternativeTo](https://alternativeto.net/software/shazam/)** — Add Lyrical as alternative
- **[Lyrically on AlternativeTo](https://alternativeto.net/software/lyrically/)** — Add Lyrical as alternative
- **[Spotify on AlternativeTo](https://alternativeto.net/software/spotify/)** — Add Lyrical as alternative (specifically for the lyrics feature)
- **[MiniLyrics on AlternativeTo](https://alternativeto.net/software/minilyrics/)** — Add Lyrical as alternative

Also create Lyrical's own listing on AlternativeTo at [alternativeto.net/add/](https://alternativeto.net/add/) if not already done.

### K-Pop & Asian Press

Lyrical's K-Pop use case is the differentiated angle that general music apps can't match. These publications have dedicated tech/app coverage:

- **[Soompi](https://www.soompi.com/)** — Largest English K-Pop news site. Pitch a "best apps for K-Pop fans" feature. Press contact via [soompi.com/about](https://www.soompi.com/about).
- **[Allkpop](https://www.allkpop.com/)** — High-traffic K-Pop media. Tech/apps column exists; pitch a feature on lyric learning tools.
- **[KpopStarz](https://www.kpopstarz.com/)** — Smaller but easier to place. App reviews accepted.
- **[AsianFanatics Forums](https://asianfanatics.net/)** — Forum community; authentic mentions in relevant threads

### Music Tech Publications

- **[MusicTech](https://www.musictech.net/)** — UK-based music technology publication. App reviews section; pitch a profile of Lyrical's translation and learning features.
- **[CDM (Create Digital Music)](https://cdm.link/)** — Music tech blog with engaged audience. Less commercial, more likely to cover a genuinely useful independent app.
- **[Hypebot](https://www.hypebot.com/)** — Music industry publication; covers apps and tools that serve artists and fans.

## Throwback

Throwback competes in the photo/nostalgia app space, which has well-established publication coverage and active AlternativeTo listings. The combination of photography publications and app comparison sites is the core strategy here.

### Photo App Directories

- **[AlternativeTo — Throwback page](https://alternativeto.net/add/)** — Create listing if not done; then add Throwback as alternative on Unfolder, Cluster, and other photo memory app pages
- **[AppAdvice](https://appadvice.com/)** — Apple-focused app directory; submit for editorial consideration
- **[148Apps](https://www.148apps.com/)** — iOS app reviews; reach out for inclusion in photo app roundups
- **[iPhoneLife](https://www.iphonelife.com/)** — Large iOS-focused publication; "best photo apps" roundups are a recurring content format

### Photography Publications

- **[PetaPixel](https://petapixel.com/)** (DA 79) — Top photography publication. "Best iOS apps for photographers" features appear regularly. Submit a press kit and pitch inclusion.
- **[Digital Photography Review (dpreview)](https://www.dpreview.com/)** — Now archive-only (Amazon shut it down) but its backlinks remain. Target sites that cited dpreview's app roundups — they're now gap opportunities.
- **[Digital Photo World](https://www.digitalphotoworld.com/)** — UK photography magazine; app reviews accepted
- **[Photography Life](https://photographylife.com/)** — Independent photo education site; covers apps and tools

### AlternativeTo Strategy

Same approach as Lyrical. Add Throwback as an alternative on competing photo memory/nostalgia app pages:

- **1 Second Everyday** on AlternativeTo — Add Throwback as alternative
- **Cluster** on AlternativeTo — Add Throwback as alternative
- **Unfolder** on AlternativeTo — Add Throwback as alternative
- **Slidebox** on AlternativeTo — Add Throwback as alternative
